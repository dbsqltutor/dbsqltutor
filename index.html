About db.sql.tutor

My name is Rene Cloutier and I am a senior-level Database Administrator, current college facilitator at Algonquin College, and independent SQL/database tutor. I bring extensive real‑world experience helping students move from "confusion" to "clarity".

My focus is simple: clear explanations, real-world reasoning, and helping you understand why SQL works the way it does — not just how to write a query.

What I help with

  • SQL fundamentals: queries, JOINs, filtering, grouping, subqueries
  • Database design: modeling, tables, keys, relationships, normalization
  • Logic: requirements, problem-solving
  • Real-world reasoning: translating questions into queries
  • Performance & troubleshooting: query tuning basics, intro to execution plans, optimization, indexing, query efficiency
  • Assignments & projects: understanding requirements and designing solutions

Who I work with

  • College and university students who want to truly understand the material
  • Career changers moving into data or development roles
  • Junior developers who need stronger SQL and database foundations
  • Professionals who “get by” with SQL but want to feel confident

No judgment—just clear explanations, patient guidance, and a structured path forward.
